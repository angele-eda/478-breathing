About Breath24

Breath24 is a lightweight collection of breathing exercise timers. The goal is simple: fast to open, easy to use, and minimal distractions.

Use it for sleep (4-7-8), focus (box breathing), a quick stress reset (4-4-6), daily balance (5-5), or gentle relaxation (belly / alternate nostril).

Why it’s different

No complex setup. No account. Just clean cues and a timer that works on mobile and desktop.
