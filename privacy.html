Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Breath24 is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly. This page explains what information may be collected and how it is used.

1) Information we collect

  • Personal information: We do not require accounts and we do not ask you to submit personal information to use the timers.
  • Usage/analytics: If analytics are enabled in the future, we may collect basic usage data (e.g., page views) to improve the site.
  • Device data: Your browser may automatically send technical data (e.g., user-agent, IP) as part of standard web requests.

2) Cookies

Breath24 does not intentionally set tracking cookies for basic use. If analytics or third-party services are added later, those services may use cookies.

3) Third-party services

If we use third-party services (e.g., analytics, ads, or embedded assets), they may process limited data as part of providing their service. We will update this policy if such services are added.

4) Data retention

If any logs or analytics exist, data may be retained for a limited period for security and performance monitoring.

5) Contact

If you have questions about privacy, you can add a contact method later (email/contact form).

Note: This is a general, lightweight privacy policy for a static website. If you add ads/analytics or collect user input later, update this page accordingly.
